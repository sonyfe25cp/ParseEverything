    SUKUK MURABAHAH : PRASARANA ISSUES RM2 BILLION SUKUK FOR CAPEX AND WORKING CAPITAL
    4 Mar 2014, 05:00pm BY SYARIFAH RAHMAN

    Syarikat Prasarana Negara Bhd has issued a 2 billion ringgit in nominal value sukuk murabahah.

    Prasarana said proceeds from the sukuk would be used as capital expenditure and general working capital purposes.

    The sukuk, comprising 1.5 billion ringgit of five-year tranche and 500 million ringgit of 10-year, is guaranteed by the government.

    In a statement today, Prasarana said the sukuk carried a semi-annual profit rate of 4.08 per cent per annum for the five-year tranche.

    And 4.67 per cent per annum for the 10-year tranche.

    Prasarana said the offering met with demand from a diverse range of investors and allocated to a mix of government agencies, financial institutions, fund managers, insurance companies and corporate accounts.
