    HAZY DAYS : SCHOOLS TO REOPEN TOMORROW
    16 Mar 2014, 06:54pm BY SYARIFAH RAHMAN

    All 202 schools closed, due to the hazardous level of haze on Friday, will be reopened tomorrow.

    Second Deputy Education Minister, P. Kamalanathan said the latest Air Pollutant Index, API reading showed air quality was safe and all schooling activities can resume as normal.    

    However, principals and headmasters have been directed to adhere to the circular on haze issued by the ministry earlier this month. 

    The Education Ministry had earlier ordered all schools to be closed if the API reading exceeded 200 which is a very unhealthy level.  

    Last week, the Selangor Education Department directed 131 schools in the Klang district and 71 schools in Kuala Langat to close on Friday following the API reading breaching very unhealthy levels in the two districts.
